Dice Game

How to play

This game allows two users to play against each other. The goal of the game is to be the first player to acquire thirty points. Both players start with zero points and take turns. When it is Player 1's turn, they can throw the pair of dice as many times as they want, adding the total of each throw to their score. Player 1 can choose to stop throwing at any point, if they stop throwing then their current score is added to their overall score. If Player 1 does not stop and throws one of the dice with a six, then Player 1's turn ends and Player 1 gets 0 added to their overall score. If Player 1 is still throwing and they throw double sixes, then Player 1's overall total score goes back to 0. (i.e., not only does Player 1 lose the scores from this round but they lose all the points they have accumulated in previous rounds). You may choose to have the double sixes rule removed and let a double six be the same as a single six by checking the box below.
